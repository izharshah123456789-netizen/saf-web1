About Safshikan

Redefining Agri-Tech
in Punjab

Founded in Punjab, Pakistan, Safshikan bridges the gap between modern drone technology and traditional farming. Our certified operators work alongside local farmers to deliver measurable results — higher yields, reduced input costs, and sustainable practices.

With a fleet of advanced agricultural drones and a team of experienced agri-tech professionals, we have serviced over 247 farms across Punjab, covering more than 12,000 acres with precision aerial solutions.
